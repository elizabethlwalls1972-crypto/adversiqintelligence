## World-First OS Advancement Path

What would make this genuinely undiscovered territory — things no product on earth currently does in combination:

### 01 — Live Adversarial Calibration

Instead of static persona weights, let the 5-persona debate adjust its skepticism levels based on the outcome track record of previous analyses. 

If the Skeptic was wrong 70% of the time in a given sector, its weight drops. If the Optimist proved correct 90% of the time in infrastructure, its weight rises.

**No commercial system does this.**

**Implementation:** `services/calibration/LiveAdversarialCalibration.ts` — tracks prediction history, recalibrates weights based on sectoral/regional accuracy.

### 02 — Structural Twin Discovery Engine

Your RegionalMirroringEngine already seeds this. Productize it.

Given any regional brief, automatically surface the 3 most structurally similar regions globally, show what happened to them, and extract the transferable lessons. 

*"Vietnam's manufacturing growth curve mirrors Poland's 15 years ago. Here's what Poland's policymakers did next. Here's what failed. Here's what you can copy."*

**This is a standalone product.**

**Implementation:** `services/twins/StructuralTwinDiscoveryEngine.ts` — 50+ regional profiles, 19-dimension similarity weighting, case study extraction.

### 04 — Confidence Calibration as a Trust Signal

Your Pattern Confidence Engine already classifies outputs as Authoritative / Informed / Exploratory / Speculative.
